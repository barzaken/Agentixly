## Why AI Leads Convert Better

Think about the psychology of the user.
* **Google Search:** The user has to click, read, compare, and decide.
* **AI Answer:** The user receives a synthesized recommendation.

When an AI tool mentions your brand, it acts as a **third-party validation**. The trust factor is higher, and therefore, the conversion rate is higher.
